Frequently Asked Questions

Your notes are saved locally in your browser's LocalStorage. This means they stay on your device and aren't sent to any server.

The 5 starter notes only appear on your very first visit. Once you delete them, they stay gone to keep your workspace yours!

Click the "+" button to see the color options. Pick a color circle to spawn a new note, or use the Multi-color icon to choose a custom shade.

Click the Heart (❤️) icon on any note to mark it as a favorite. You can then use the "Favourites" filter at the top to see only your starred notes.

Use the Search Bar at the top. It filters your notes in real-time based on the text inside them. It even works combined with your category filters!

Click the Trash Can icon at the bottom right of any note. Careful—this action is permanent and cannot be undone!
